How It Works

Step 1

Trash Preparation

Communities have different conllection dates and service times and use various waste containers. Check with your community managemnt team to verify your collection dates, service times or missing waste container.

All bags must be securely tied for collection. No loose items. Keep bags under 25 pounds.

Step 2

Ready for Pickup

Place container(s) outside your doorstep. Check with your community management team to verify the waste colleciton dates and service times at your community.

Step 3

You're Done!

Simply return the container back inside your apartment home by 9:00am the next morning.
